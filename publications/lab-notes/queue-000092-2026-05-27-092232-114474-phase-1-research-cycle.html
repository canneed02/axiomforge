### 1. Falsifiable Claim

**Claim:** An autonomous research harness that maintains structured memory of its own experimental history produces measurably higher consistency in hypothesis generation than an equivalent stateless harness, as quantified by semantic coherence scores against a held-out ground-truth causal graph (target: >15% improvement on a standardized benchmark suite, e.g., modified SciWorld or a synthetic discovery environment).

*Falsification condition:* No statistically significant difference (p > 0.05) in coherence scores after controlling for compute budget and search depth.

### 2. Experiment or Proof Harness

**Harness:** `autonome-mem-v0`

- **Core loop:** LLM/planner proposes action → execution environment returns observation → memory module compresses and stores (observation, inference, confidence) as a structured entry → retrieval-augmented context fed back to proposer.
- **Memory architecture:** Dual-store: (a) episodic buffer (raw traces, last-k) + (b) semantic index (condensed causal assertions, vector-searchable).
- **Evaluation protocol:** Fixed suite of 50 synthetic discovery tasks with known causal structures; measure: (i) task success rate, (ii) hypothesis coherence (BERTScore against ground-truth edges), (iii) redundant experiment rate.

---

### 3. Three Failure Modes

| ID | Failure Mode | Detection Trigger | Mitigation (Testable) |
|---|---|---|---|
| FM-1 | **Memory hallucination:** Stored "facts" drift from actual observations due to compression or generation errors. | Audit sample: cosine similarity between stored assertion and source observation < θ. | Grounded compression: require verifiable spans; store provenance pointers. |
| FM-2 | **Retrieval collapse:** Semantic index overfits to frequent keywords, retrieving same entries regardless of query relevance. | Query-result diversity score (entropy of retrieved IDs) < threshold across task distribution. | Hard negative mining during index construction; explicit diversity regularization. |
| FM-3 | **Competence overhang:** Harness defers to memory rather than executing new experiments, even when memory is stale or inapplicable. | Action distribution skew: P(reuse_memory) > 0.9 with no performance gain over 10 steps. | Uncertainty-gated exploration: force physical experiment when epistemic uncertainty > bound. |
